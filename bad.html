2019 All England Open Badminton Championships

The 2019 All England open was the first time in 16 years that either Lin Dan, Chen Long, Lee Chong Wei did not contest the final.

2019 French Open Badminton Championships

Chen Long became the first badminton player to defend his title at the French Open Badminton Championships since the tournament's inception in 1909.

2025 BWF World Championships

Kunlavut Vitidsarn contested three consecutive finals at the BWF World Championships, winning the title in 2023 and finishing runner-up in 2025 and 2022. Only Three players have contested three consecutive finals at the BWF World Championships, they are Lin Dan, Lee Chong Wei and Kunlavut Vitidsarn.
